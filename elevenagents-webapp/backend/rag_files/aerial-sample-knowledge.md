## About Aerial

Aerial is a premium international airline headquartered at Singapore Changi
Airport (SIN), serving the global premium travel segment. Aerial operates three
cabins: **Business**, **First**, and the flagship **Aerial Suites** — private
double-room suites available on selected long-haul aircraft.

## Cabins

- **Business** — fully flat 1.9 m bed, direct-aisle access on all seats,
  18-inch HD screen, on-demand dining, priority boarding and baggage.
- **First** — 2.1 m bed, sliding privacy doors, noise-cancelling headsets,
  caviar service, and chauffeur transfers in 60+ cities.
- **Aerial Suites** — a private double-room suite with a separate bed and chair,
  a wardrobe, and a 32-inch screen. Companions travelling together can convert
  two suites into a double bed.

## Baggage Policy (sample)

- **Business:** 2 checked bags up to 32 kg each; 2 cabin bags up to 7 kg each.
- **First & Aerial Suites:** 3 checked bags up to 32 kg each; 2 cabin bags up
  to 7 kg each.
- **AerialClub Gold/Diamond:** +1 additional checked bag.
- Excess baggage can be pre-purchased up to 24 hours before departure at a
  discount versus airport rates.

## Lounges

- **The Aerial Private Room (SIN):** reserved for First and Aerial Suites
  guests; à la carte dining, private shower suites, and a quiet cabin.
- **Aerial Business Lounge (SIN):** for Business guests and AerialClub Gold+;
  buffet and à la carte, barista bar, and shower suites.
- Partner lounges are available at 90+ international airports.

## AerialClub Loyalty (sample)

Tiers: **Blue → Silver → Gold → Diamond.**

- Miles are earned on all Aerial and partner flights; premium cabins earn a
  cabin bonus (Business 125%, First 150%, Suites 200%).
- **Gold** unlocks lounge access, priority boarding, and an extra checked bag.
- **Diamond** adds guaranteed award seats, complimentary upgrades on eligible
  fares, and a personal travel concierge line.
- Miles do not expire while the account has qualifying activity every 18 months.

## Booking & Changes (sample)

- Flexible premium fares allow free date changes up to 24 hours before
  departure; fare difference may apply.
- Refundable fares can be cancelled for a full refund to the original payment
  method.
- Seat selection is complimentary in all cabins.
- Chauffeur transfers (First / Suites) should be booked at least 24 hours
  before departure via the AerialClub app or the concierge line.
